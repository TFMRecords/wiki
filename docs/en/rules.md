# General Submission Rules

- Must be in a records room, rooms like tribe house\* and utility not allowed!
- If in a regular bootcamp, racing or hardcamp room, video is required.
  - Format of submission for these records is `!submit Username#0000 @8000000 10.00`
- Records made in a regular or vanilla rooms cannot be submitted (shaman abilities and throwables), we may allow exceptions (when those don't interfere)
- All submissions made in records room require **[submithash](../module.md)**
- Screenshots require being **fullscreen**, this means whole screen including taskbar
- Use of third party programs like filterkeys/macro and other cheats or hacks are prohibited
- No use of vps or private ping 0 servers
- **/ips** must be enabled
- The standalones where screenshot and video are allowed: **[transformice.exe and steam](../clients.md)**
- The standalones where only video is allowed: **baffler** and **adobe flash player 10**
- Account must be at least 6 months old
- Glitches like x bug and spawn with cheese bug are prohibited
- View the blacklist rules below

\* See below for exceptions

# Parkour Submission Rules

- Parkour records must be done in `#parkour0records Username#0000` rooms
- All **checkpoint times** must be disclosed in your proof screenshot/video or in a separate message
- Format of submission is `!submit Username#0000 @8000000 10.00`

# Module Submission Rules

- Module off category maps can just be played in records rooms and be submitted like regular records
- All module on submissions require **video**
- All module on records must be made in a **module room** or in **tribe house** (e.g. `#playground`)
- In Circuit **Power Ups are forbidden**, if you pick up a power up your run will be invalid

\* For ninja use `#ninja0editor` to load a map, for reversed maps use tribehouse
\* To load modules in tribe house type `/module ModuleName`
\* To load a map in tribe house type `/np @1234`
\* To exit out of the module, load another module or type `/module stop`
