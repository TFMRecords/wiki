Welcome to the TFM Records Wiki.

This guide will help you understand how to participate, record your runs properly, and submit them correctly.

Whether you're new or experienced, please follow the steps below to make sure your runs are valid.

---

## Find a map to play

- Browse maps on our [website](https://www.tfmrecords.com)
- Find a map of your choice
- Copy the map code (e.g. @7975329)
- Check out rules for that category

---
