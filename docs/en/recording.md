title: Setup Recording
icon: material/record-circle
---

Ensure your gameplay is recorded properly:

- Record the full run
- Show required information on screen
- Avoid cuts or edits
- Use the correct settings
- Ensure your recording starts **before your mouse spawns** and ends **after the mouse enters the hole**, so the run is measured accurately.

Follow this [guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1HPrDQKnv562DH90seHQ1Bi5VVrDzwZP_HkTaCBwTU0U/edit)
